How Does it Work?

Decide to be an expert adviser or the one who receives advice


Advisees post a question in a specific category and select whether or not they want it to be private


Mentors select a question they're interested in advising and get paired with the advisee who asked it


Match and start chatting!
Why Use Advice Giver?

Counseling without the fees - personable 1-on-1 support
Grow as a Person - advisor and advisees can benefit from each other and both come out of the exchange feeling better about themselves
Zero risk invovled - advisees can choose to be private and also unmatch with their mentor at anytime, mitigating risk or concerns
