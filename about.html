About Us

Our mission

Improving quality of life by providing a safe, comfortable, and convienent platform to both seek and provide advice.

How we achieve that mission

By constantly looking for ways to optimize the user experience - emphasizing both privacy and community throughout the process.

Meet the Team

We are proud Lambda School students inspired by the mission to help solve or alleviate societal problems through technology. We decided to build Advice-Giver with hopes that we can make an impact on individuals in need of help that don't have a platform they feel 100% comfortable using yet.
